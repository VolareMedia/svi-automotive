What We Do

Our Services

Equipment Installation

Professional installation of vehicle lifts, tire changers, and wheel balancers. Every install is anchored, leveled, tested, and signed off to manufacturer spec before we leave your shop.

Mobile Repair

Equipment breaks down — we come to you. Our mobile repair service covers all of Vancouver Island so you're not waiting on a mainland tech or trucking your equipment off-site.

Alignment Machine Service

Alignment machines need regular calibration and maintenance to stay accurate. We service and recalibrate alignment equipment to keep your readings trustworthy and your customers satisfied.

AC Machine Repairs

AC machine issues sideline a revenue-generating service. We diagnose and repair AC equipment on-site — fast, so you can get back to offering your customers the full range of services they expect.

Lift Inspections & Recertification

Annual lift inspections and ALI recertification keep your equipment legal, safe, and covered by warranty. We handle the paperwork and the work — you just keep the shop moving.

Ongoing Support

Bought your equipment elsewhere and need support? If we can help, we will. Over 20 years on the island means we've seen most makes and models and know how to get them running right.

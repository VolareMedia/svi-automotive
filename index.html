FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you install the equipment you sell?

Yes. We professionally install vehicle lifts, tire changers, and wheel balancers, and we assess your space (ceiling height, floor, and power) before you buy so there are no surprises on install day.

What areas do you serve?

We serve all of Greater Victoria and Vancouver Island — including Victoria, Saanich, Saanichton, Langford, Sidney, Duncan, Nanaimo, Courtenay, and Campbell River — with mobile sales, installation, and service.

Are you an authorized Atlas dealer?

Yes. SVI Automotive Equipment is an authorized Atlas Automotive Equipment dealer. Every Atlas product is ALI-certified where applicable and backed by manufacturer warranty plus our local service.

Do you offer financing?

Yes — $0-down lease-to-own financing through Lease Link Canada, with terms up to 60 months. See financing options.

Do you repair and service equipment too?

Absolutely. We offer mobile repair and service for lifts, tire changers, balancers, alignment machines, and AC machines across the Island. Learn about our service.

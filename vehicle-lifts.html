FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Should I choose a 2-post or 4-post lift?

It depends on the work you do — 2-post lifts give open underbody access for general repair, while 4-post lifts are more stable and ideal for alignments, heavier trucks, and storage. Read our full 2-post vs 4-post guide.

Are your vehicle lifts ALI certified?

Yes. Our Atlas lifts are ALI-certified where applicable, meeting recognized North American safety and performance standards.

Do you install the lifts you sell?

Yes. Professional installation is part of what we do. We confirm your ceiling height, floor slab, and electrical supply beforehand so the lift is installed safely and correctly.

What warranty comes with Atlas lifts?

Atlas lifts are backed by manufacturer warranty — the Atlas Platinum PVL series, for example, carries a 5-year structural warranty. We also back every install with local service.

Can I finance a vehicle lift?

Yes. You can finance any lift with $0 down through Lease Link Canada. See financing options.

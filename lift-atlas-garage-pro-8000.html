Warranty Information

Every Atlas lift supplied and installed by SVI Automotive Equipment is covered by the Atlas manufacturer warranty and backed by our local service across Vancouver Island.

Standard Manufacturer Warranty

  • 5 years on structural components
  • 2 years on hydraulic cylinders
  • 1 year on electric / air-hydraulic power units
  • 90 days on wear items

Optional Extended Warranty

Atlas offers two extended-warranty programs you can add at purchase, covering parts including hydraulic hoses, fittings, pumps, and cylinders:

  • BLUE 2+ — 2-year extended coverage
  • PLATINUM 3+ — 3-year extended coverage

Ask us which program fits your shop. Warranty terms are set by Atlas Automotive Equipment and subject to their terms and conditions; SVI Automotive Equipment handles local warranty service and support.

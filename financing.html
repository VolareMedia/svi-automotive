FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does lease-to-own financing work?

You choose your equipment, complete a quick application, and once approved you make affordable monthly payments over a set term — at the end of the term the equipment is yours. Read the full explainer.

Is there really $0 down?

Yes. $0-down lease-to-own options are available so you can preserve your cash flow and put the equipment to work right away.

What credit do I need to qualify?

We work with most credit types — strong or challenged — and we finance new businesses, not just established shops. Financing is subject to credit approval for qualifying Canadian businesses.

What equipment can I finance?

Vehicle lifts, tire changers, wheel balancers, alignment equipment, and full shop packages can all be rolled into one financing agreement.

Will it affect my personal credit?

This is business financing through Lease Link Canada and does not report to your personal credit. Lease payments may also offer tax advantages — check with your accountant.

How do I apply?

Apply online in just a few minutes, or reach out and we will bring financing into the same conversation as your equipment quote. Contact us.

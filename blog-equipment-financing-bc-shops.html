$0-Down Lease-to-Own,
Explained for BC Shops

Equip your shop now and pay over time — here's how lease-to-own financing works, what you can finance, and who qualifies.

Financing June 20, 2026 4 min read

Outfitting a shop is expensive. A single two-post lift, a tire changer, and a balancer can add up fast — and dropping that much cash all at once can leave you short for payroll, parts, and the next opportunity. That's why more BC shop owners are financing their equipment instead of buying outright.

Why Lease Instead of Buy Outright?

Paying cash ties up money you could be using to run and grow your business. Lease-to-own financing lets you put that equipment to work earning revenue immediately while spreading the cost into predictable monthly payments. The lift starts paying for itself from day one.

How Lease-to-Own Works

It's simple: you choose your equipment, we send you a quick application, and once approved you make affordable monthly payments over a set term. At the end of the term, the equipment is yours. SVI Automotive Equipment partners with Lease Link Canada to make the process fast and straightforward.

  • $0 down lease-to-own options to preserve your cash flow
  • Terms up to 60 months to keep monthly payments manageable
  • Fast approvals and competitive rates
  • Does not report to your personal credit

What You Can Finance

Just about everything we sell. Vehicle lifts, tire changers, wheel balancers, alignment equipment, and full shop packages can all be rolled into one financing agreement — so you can build out a complete bay in a single conversation rather than buying piece by piece.

Built for BC Businesses

  • We work with most credit types — strong or challenged
  • We finance new businesses, not just established shops
  • Lease payments may offer tax advantages (check with your accountant)
  • One simple monthly payment instead of a large upfront hit

Who Qualifies and How to Apply

Financing is available to qualifying Canadian businesses and is subject to credit approval. Applying takes just a few minutes online, and you can have a decision quickly so you're not left waiting to get your bay running. Spec your equipment with us and we'll bring financing into the same conversation.
